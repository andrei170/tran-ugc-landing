FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need followers to start?

No. UGC is about portfolio, not following. Most of my students land their first paid deal with under 1,000 followers.

Do I need to be on camera?

No. Many successful UGC creators use voiceover, product shots, or hands-only content. Face-on camera is a stylistic choice, not a requirement.

How much time per day?

Most students spend 1-2 hours/day in the first 90 days (pitching + portfolio work). Once booked deals come in, time shifts to content creation. Usually 2-4 hours/day at $5K+/month tier.

How long until I'm paid?

Most students land their first paid deal within 30-60 days of starting Foundations. First consistent $2K+/month typically months 4-6.

Do you work with men?

Yes. Most of my community is women (especially healthcare workers) but men are welcome. The framework is gender-neutral — the community just skews one way.
